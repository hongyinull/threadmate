# Threadmate Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-30_

Threadmate is a browser extension that drafts AI replies and shows local insights for Threads and X. It is built to collect and transmit as little as possible.

## What Threadmate does NOT do
- It has **no backend server**. There is no Threadmate server that receives your data.
- It does **not** track you, run analytics, or send telemetry.
- It does **not** sell, rent, or share your data with anyone for advertising or any unrelated purpose.

## Data stored locally (on your device only)
Stored in your browser's extension storage and never sent to us:
- Your AI provider API key(s) (OpenAI / Anthropic / Google Gemini).
- Your preferences (tone, language, templates, promo text, auto-interaction settings).
- Local usage counts (drafts generated/inserted), for your own reference.

## Data sent to the AI provider you choose
When you generate a reply, Threadmate sends the text of the post you are replying to (plus your style settings) **directly from your browser to the AI provider you configured**, using **your own API key**. This request does **not** pass through any Threadmate server. The provider's handling of that data is governed by that provider's own privacy policy.

## Page content
Threadmate reads the content of the Threads/X page you are viewing to extract the post you want to reply to and to operate its features. This happens locally in your browser and is not transmitted anywhere, except — when you generate a reply — to your chosen AI provider as described above.

## Your control
- Remove your API keys and all settings any time from the Options page, or by removing the extension.
- Threadmate is open source — you can inspect exactly what it does.
